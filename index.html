The 2026 Regulatory Wave

Four mandates. Zero margin for error.

European carriers face simultaneous compliance deadlines in 2026. Miss one and your vehicles get detained, fines pile up, and routes close.

ADR Certificate Renewal

June 2026

Hazardous goods drivers must hold a valid ADR certificate. Expired cert = vehicle detained at border inspection. No grace period in NL, DE, IT, BE.

ICS2 Border Declaration

Live Now · NL / DE / CZ

Pre-arrival customs filing required before entering the EU. Violations carry a €5,000 fine per shipment. Already active in the Netherlands, Germany, and Czech Republic.

Smart Tachograph G2V2

July 1, 2026

Second-generation smart tachographs become mandatory for vans and light commercial vehicles. Working time data must be digitally logged and accessible to inspectors.

eCMR Digital Standard

2027 Mandate

Paper CMR is becoming obsolete. EU eCMR regulation requires digital consignment notes with verifiable audit trails. Start building your digital archive now — before the deadline.
