01 CMR Digital Scan

Photo in. Validated data out. TMS synced.

Driver photographs the signed, dated CMR after delivery. AI reads up to 45 fields across 24 UNECE boxes in under 30 seconds. If a field is missing or the image is illegible, the process rejects and requests a retake. Once confirmed, data is pushed to your TMS or ERP — with zero manual entry.

Extracted automatically:

✓ Sender & Consignee ✓ Goods & weight ✓ Pickup & delivery dates ✓ Vehicle plates ✓ ADR hazard class ✓ All 4 signature boxes
up to 45 fields 5 languages TMS / ERP sync <1 min integration
02 Vehicle Inspection Checklist

Full inspection. Zero paperwork.

TruckPilot guides the driver through every inspection point via conversation — no forms, no clipboards. Standard template covers 35 items, ADR loads get 42 items with hazard class validation against the driver's registered certificate. At the end, a timestamped PDF report is generated automatically — available for download or included in your compliance dashboard.

The generated report includes:

✓ Date, time & driver ✓ Vehicle plate ✓ All checked items ✓ Failed items + comments ✓ ADR load validation ✓ GPS location (optional)
35 / 42 items ADR template PDF export Audit-ready

04 Cargo Release PIN

Prove who picks up. Eliminate disputes.

Before cargo is released, the shipper registers a 6-digit PIN in TruckPilot. At the delivery point, the driver enters the PIN — which is cross-verified between both parties in real time. This creates an immutable audit trail proving that the right carrier collected the right load.

The double-check flow:

1 Shipper registers PIN for delivery #4821
2 Driver arrives → TruckPilot requests PIN
3 PIN matched → carrier identity verified
4 Both parties receive confirmation + timestamp
6-digit PIN 72h validity Immutable audit trail Fraud prevention

05 ADR Certificate Control

Never miss a renewal. Never lose a load.

Driver photographs front and back of the ADR certificate. TruckPilot extracts holder name, authorized hazard classes, and expiry — then monitors it automatically. Renewal reminders escalate at D-30, D-20, D-10, D-5, then daily. Driver triggers renewal directly from WhatsApp. Fleet manager receives every critical alert.

Without this control:

Expired ADR = vehicle detained at inspection. In NL, DE, IT, BE: zero grace period. Load misses delivery window. Fine + recovery cost + client penalty.

Photo extraction Escalating alerts Manager notification Renewal flow built-in
06 ADR Roadside Verification

Police stop. Inspector report ready in 20 seconds.

When stopped by police or a transport authority, the driver attaches the ADR verification document. TruckPilot checks every conformity — certificate validity, authorized classes, vehicle equipment, cargo vs. certificate match — and generates a structured verification report immediately.

Verification covers:

✓ Certificate validity ✓ Authorized classes ✓ Vehicle equipment ✓ Cargo vs. cert match ✓ Emergency contacts ✓ Safety placards
Structured PDF report Instant conformity check Saved to compliance log
