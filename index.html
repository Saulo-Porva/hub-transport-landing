01 CMR Digital Scan

Photo in. Validated CMR out. TMS updated.

Driver photographs the signed and dated CMR after delivery — that's it. AI reads all 45 fields across 24 UNECE boxes in under 30 seconds. If anything is missing or illegible, the process rejects the image and asks for a retake. Once confirmed with OK, data is pushed directly to your TMS or ERP.

What gets extracted automatically:

✓ Sender & Consignee✓ Goods description ✓ Weight & volume✓ Pickup & delivery dates ✓ Vehicle plates✓ ADR hazard class ✓ Freight payment✓ All 4 signature boxes

04 Cargo Release PIN

Double verification. Zero disputes.

Before releasing cargo, the shipper registers a 6-digit PIN in TruckPilot. At the delivery point, the driver enters the PIN — which is then cross-checked between the client and the carrier in real time. Both sides confirm the handover, creating an immutable digital audit trail. No more "I never received it" disputes or fraudulent pickups.

How the double-check works:

1. Shipper registers PIN for delivery #4821
2. Driver arrives — TruckPilot requests the PIN
3. Driver enters PIN — system verifies identity of carrier
4. Both parties receive confirmation + timestamp
6-digit PIN 72h validity Double-check Immutable audit trail Fraud prevention

05 ADR Certificate Control

Never miss a renewal. Never lose a load.

Driver photographs the front and back of the ADR certificate. TruckPilot extracts the holder, authorized hazard classes, and expiry date — then tracks it automatically. Renewal reminders are sent at D-30, D-20, D-10, D-5, and daily from D-1 onward. The driver can trigger the renewal process directly from the WhatsApp chat. Fleet manager is notified at every critical stage.

What happens without this:

Expired ADR certificate = vehicle detained at border inspection. No cargo movement. Fine + recovery cost. In NL, DE and IT: no grace period.

Photo extraction Escalating alerts Manager notification Renewal flow Classes 1–9 tracked

The 2026 Regulatory Wave

Four mandates. Zero margin for error.

European carriers face simultaneous compliance deadlines in 2026. Miss one and vehicles get detained, fines pile up, and routes close.

ADR Certificate Renewal

June 2026

Hazardous goods drivers must hold valid ADR certification. Expired cert = vehicle detained at inspection. No grace period in NL, DE, IT, BE.

ICS2 Border Declaration

Live Now

Pre-arrival customs filing required. Violations: €5,000 fine per shipment. Already active in Netherlands, Germany, Czech Republic.

Smart Tachograph G2V2

July 1, 2026

Mandatory for vans and light commercial vehicles. Working time data must be digitally logged and accessible to inspectors.

eCMR Digital Standard

2027 Mandate

Paper CMR becoming obsolete. EU eCMR regulation requires digital consignment notes with verifiable audit trails. Start building your archive now.
